\# 🛒 Retail Sales Analysis | SQL, Excel \& Power BI



\## 📌 Project Overview



This project analyzes a retail sales dataset to uncover valuable business insights using \*\*SQL\*\*, \*\*Excel\*\*, and \*\*Power BI\*\*.



The objective is to answer real-world business questions related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends.



This project simulates the work performed by a Data Analyst in a business environment.

\# 📈 Key Business Insights



\- Technology generated the highest overall profit.

\- Consumer segment contributed the highest sales.

\- November recorded the highest sales.

\- California generated the highest sales among all states.

\- New York City generated the highest city-wise sales.

\- Technology achieved the highest profit margin among all categories.
